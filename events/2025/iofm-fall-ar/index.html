Why RFPs Are Holding AR Teams Back

Legacy RFP cycles slow down adoption and leave stakeholders disengaged. We will demonstrate how to replace multi-month paper processes with rapid pilots that surface the right partners and data-backed ROI.

  • 1. The Case Against Traditional RFPs

    Benchmark how long cycles create false confidence, incomplete requirements, and poor vendor accountability.

  • 2. Pilot-First Evaluation

    See a playbook for scoping a 90-day pilot that uncovers integration considerations and success metrics.

  • 3. Change-Ready Stakeholder Alignment

    Learn facilitation tactics to get sales, finance, and operations aligned on AR automation outcomes.
