Three Segments

Every treasury solution fits into one of these segments, each designed for different organizational needs and complexity levels.

Cash Tools

Cash Tools provide visibility into your money. They connect to your banks, pull in transaction data, and help you see where your cash is across accounts and entities. They include cash forecasting to help you project future cash positions.

Best For
Companies with simple treasury needs with a few banks, needing better visibility and forecasting without more complex payment or risk requirements.
Core Capabilities
  • Bank connectivity
  • Global cash visibility
  • Transaction reporting (search, sort, filter, tag)
  • Cash forecasting
Key Question: "How much cash do we have, where is it, and what is it going to be next month?"

You're in this segment if: You need bank connectivity + cash visibility + cash forecasting + transaction reporting ONLY.
‚ö°

TMS-Lite

TMS-Lite adds payment capabilities on top of visibility. Beyond seeing your cash, you can now move it -- initiating, approving, and transmitting payments through structured workflows.

Best For
Companies with growing treasury complexity and multi-entity structures needing payment automation, approval workflows, and operational treasury controls.
Core Capabilities (includes Cash Tools, plus)
  • Payment initiation
  • Payment approval workflows
  • Payment file transmission
Key Question: "How do we efficiently manage and move our cash?"

You're in this segment if: You need everything in Cash Tools PLUS payment initiation, approval, and payment transmission.
üè¢

TRMS

Treasury & Risk Management Systems

TRMS is full-spectrum treasury technology that adds financial instrument trading and risk management for a complete treasury operations infrastructure. This includes debt, investment, and FX deal capture and valuations.

Best For
Companies with complex treasury requirements, often with significant FX exposure, debt portfolios, and active risk management needs.
Core Capabilities (includes TMS-Lite, plus)
  • Full support for debt, investment, and FX management
  • Deal capture, accounting, and valuations
  • Risk analytics, limit tracking & exposure reporting
Key Question: "How do we run a complete, institutional-grade treasury operation?"

You're in this segment if: You need everything in TMS-Lite PLUS debt and investment management, deal capture, accounting and valuations, and risk analytics with limits and exposure reporting.
