What is a Treasury Management System?

A centralized platform that automates treasury processes by connecting data from banks and ERP systems to create insights through analytics and process automation.

A Treasury Management System serves as the hub for all treasury activity, connecting disparate financial systems to create a unified ecosystem for cash management, payments, and financial operations. By aggregating treasury data, a TMS transforms how organizations approach financial management.
