Three Segments

Every treasury solution fits into one of these segments, each designed for different organizational needs and complexity levels.

$

Cash Tools

Cash Tools provide visibility into your money. They connect to your banks, pull in transaction data, and help you see where your cash is across accounts and entities. They include cash forecasting to help you project future cash positions. Cash Tools answer the question: "How much cash do we have, where is it, and what is it going to be next month?"

Best for

Companies with simple treasury needs with a few banks, needing better visibility and forecasting without more complex payment or risk requirements.

  • Bank connectivity
  • Global cash visibility
  • Transaction reporting (search, sort, filter, tag)
  • Cash forecasting
⚡

TMS-Lite

TMS-Lite adds payment capabilities on top of visibility. Beyond seeing your cash, you can now move it — initiating, approving, and transmitting payments through structured workflows. It answers: "How do we efficiently manage and move our cash?"

Best for

Companies with growing treasury complexity and multi-entity structures needing payment automation, approval workflows, and operational treasury controls.

  • Everything in Cash Tools, plus:
  • Payment initiation
  • Payment approval workflows
  • Payment file transmission
⚙

TRMS

TRMS is full-spectrum treasury technology that adds financial instrument trading and risk management for a complete treasury operations infrastructure. This includes debt, investment, and FX deal capture and valuations. TRMS answers: "How do we run a complete, institutional-grade treasury operation?"

Best for

Companies with complex treasury requirements, often with significant FX exposure, debt portfolios, and active risk management needs.

  • Everything in TMS-Lite, plus:
  • Full support for debt, investment, and FX management
  • Deal capture, accounting, and valuations
  • Risk analytics, limit tracking & exposure reporting
