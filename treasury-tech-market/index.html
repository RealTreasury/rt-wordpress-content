Market Segments

Three distinct segments define the treasury technology market. Understand which one fits your organization so you can evaluate solutions with clarity and confidence.

Three Segments

Every treasury solution fits into one of these segments, each built for a different level of treasury complexity.

üí∞

Cash Tools

Cash Tools provide visibility into your money. They connect to your banks, pull in transaction data, and help you see where your cash is across accounts and entities. They include cash forecasting to help you project future cash positions.

Best For
Companies with simple treasury needs with a few banks, needing better visibility and forecasting without more complex payment or risk requirements.
Key Question It Answers
"How much cash do we have, where is it, and what is it going to be next month?"
‚ö°

TMS-Lite

TMS-Lite adds payment capabilities on top of visibility. Beyond seeing your cash, you can now move it‚Äîinitiating, approving, and transmitting payments through structured workflows.

Best For
Companies with growing treasury complexity and multi-entity structures needing payment automation, approval workflows, and operational treasury controls.
Key Question It Answers
"How do we efficiently manage and move our cash?"
üè¢

TRMS

Treasury & Risk Management Systems

TRMS is full-spectrum treasury technology that adds financial instrument trading and risk management for a complete treasury operations infrastructure. This includes debt, investment, and FX deal capture and valuations.

Best For
Companies with complex treasury requirements, often with significant FX exposure, debt portfolios, and active risk management needs.
Key Question It Answers
"How do we run a complete, institutional-grade treasury operation?"
