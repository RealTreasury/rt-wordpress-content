Real Treasury at the Big D Financial Conference

Event on September 12, 2024

Join us at the 2024 Big D Financial Conference in Dallas! Our Principal, Tracey Ferguson Knight, will be presenting a can't-miss session for anyone navigating the complex world of treasury technology.

Swipe Right for TMS Delight: Finding Your Perfect Match

There is not one BEST treasury management system for every company; the trick, however, is to find the best one for YOU! Have you seen the numbers? Half of all treasury system selection processes end up with “no decision.” A large number of companies that select a system have failed implementations or are unhappy with the choice they made. It doesn't have to be this way.

Just as there are new ways to find a partner in your personal life, finding your TMS partner requires a new approach. This session will focus on building a satisfying and long-lasting relationship with your TMS vendor, moving beyond the implementation to ensure ongoing success.

Speaker: Tracey Ferguson Knight (Principal @ Real Treasury)
