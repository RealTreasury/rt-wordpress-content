The Treasury Tech Landscape

The treasury technology market has exploded with innovation, creating hundreds of solutions across different categories and price points. Understanding which category fits your needs is the first step to making the right investment.

Three Categories

Every treasury solution fits into one of these categories, each designed for different organizational needs and growth stages.

💰

Cash Tools

Required Core Functions
  • Bank connectivity (API, SFTP)
  • Basic forecasting tools
  • Global cash visibility
  • Transaction search, sort, tag, group
Real Example
A $50M regional manufacturer consolidates 5 bank accounts and builds 13-week cash forecasts to improve visibility
Additional Features
    ✨

    Cash Tools focus on core functionality. Additional features are available when you upgrade to TMS-Lite or TRMS categories.

⚡

Treasury Management System Lite (TMS-Lite)

Required Core Functions
  • All Cash Tools functions
  • Cash positioning & reconciliation
  • Market data (FX spot rates)
  • Treasury payments (API or SFTP)
Real Example
A $200M retailer automates daily payments across 15 locations with structured approval workflows
Optional Enhancements
  • AI Forecasting
  • AI Insights
  • AP Payments
  • Bank Account Management
  • Basic FX (Spots, FWD)
  • Cash Accounting
  • Debt Management
  • Deal Accounting
  • In-House Banking
  • Investments
  • SWIFT Connectivity
🏢

Treasury & Risk Management Systems (TRMS)

Required Core Functions
  • All TMS-Lite functions
  • Derivatives (Interest & FX)
  • Intercompany loan tracking
  • Independent instrument valuations
Real Example
A $5B multinational manages FX exposure across 50+ entities with automated hedging strategies
Advanced Capabilities
  • Bank Fee Analysis
  • Commodities
  • Developer Portal
  • Excel Native Reporting
  • Hedge Management
  • Multilateral Netting
  • Payment Factory (POBO/ROBO)
  • Risk Management
